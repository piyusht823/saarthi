About Saarthi

Remote Health Monitoring System based on IoT.

Saarthi is Internet of Things (IoT) based system that allows healthcare providers to remotely monitor and manage the health of their patients using connected devices. The system uses sensors, wearables, and other connected devices to collect patient data, which is then transmitted in real-time to healthcare providers.

Why Us

Your Well-Being is our Main Priority.

Remote health monitoring is becoming increasingly important because it allows healthcare providers to monitor the health of patients who are not physically present in a clinical setting. This is especially important for patients who have chronic conditions or live in remote areas where access to healthcare is limited.
